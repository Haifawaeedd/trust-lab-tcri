# TRUST-LAB Research Notes

## NIST AI RMF Generative AI Profile

The NIST publication page for **Artificial Intelligence Risk Management Framework: Generative Artificial Intelligence Profile** states that the document is a **cross-sectoral profile** and a **companion resource** for **AI RMF 1.0** for generative AI. It explains that the AI RMF is intended for **voluntary use** and is meant to improve the ability of organizations to incorporate **trustworthiness considerations** into the **design, development, use, and evaluation** of AI products, services, and systems.

This is directly relevant to TRUST-LAB because it supports framing the project as a research and evaluation platform focused on trustworthy AI and risk-aware assessment, rather than a narrow hallucination checker.

## OWASP LLM / GenAI Risk Framing

An attempt to access the OWASP 2025 LLM applications resource through the browser was blocked by a browser-check page. However, the prior search results confirmed that OWASP maintains a current **Top 10 for LLM / GenAI application risks** resource that includes issues such as prompt injection, insecure output handling, training data poisoning, model denial of service, supply chain vulnerabilities, and sensitive information disclosure.

This remains useful as a positioning reference for TRUST-LAB documentation, especially in the methodology and risk framing sections.
